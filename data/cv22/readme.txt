Criminal Victimization, 2022  NCJ 307089	
	
This zip archive contains tables in individual  .csv spreadsheets	
from Criminal Victimization, 2022  NCJ 307089.  The full report including text	
and graphics in pdf format is available from: https://bjs.ojp.gov/library/publications/criminal-victimization-2022	
	
This report is one in a series.  More recent editions	
may be available. To view a list of all in the series go to	
https://bjs.ojp.gov/library/publications/list?series_filter=Criminal%20Victimization
